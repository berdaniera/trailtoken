Welcome to your virtual running crew

Trail Token is a web3 movement community that helps you be active

Let's go far together.

You like moving and being outside. You want to join a supportive run crew to move with like-minded folks and you want technology to enable your experience.

We are intentionally inclusive and we welcome runners of all speeds and sizes. We value community over competition and persistence over pace. We believe speed is an individual objective, so we have no leaderboards. If you want to race, look elsewhere. If you want good running vibes, join us. Our trail is open for every runner.

Built and governed by the community.
We're building Trail Token on web3 blocks to empower the community. We have a rough idea of how we think this should work:
  • It starts with a low-key activity logger (Trail Track) that encourages getting outside regularly.
  • It develops with community-organized NFT events (Trail Tokens) that incentivize collective movement.
  • It grows with IRL events, swag for members, community voice, ... and we need your help to figure out where it goes from there!
Join our discord to help us find the trail.
